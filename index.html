TEAM ROSTERS & MATCH SCHEDULING

Squad Composition

  • ✔ Roster Size: Register between 5 and 10 players maximum per team.
  • ✔ Active Play: A maximum of 5 active players (including the goalkeeper) on court.
  • ✔ Rolling Subs: Dynamic swaps allowed. Exiting player must fully exit the touchline before substitute enters.

Match Times & Rules

  • ✔ Weekly Fixtures: Played Monday through Thursday (18:00 - 21:00).
  • ✔ Match Duration: Two 20-minute halves divided by a 5-minute halftime.
  • ✔ Forfeit Rule: Teams must arrive 15 mins early. If 4 players are not fielded within 10 mins of start time, a forfeit is triggered (3-0 loss).

GENERAL GAMEPLAY REGULATIONS

🚨 CRITICAL SAFETY DIRECTIVE

No Slide Tackling: Sliding along the ground to challenge, intercept, or tackle an opponent is strictly banned. Any violation results in an immediate Yellow Card (2-minute suspension) and an indirect free kick awarded to the opposing side. Hard physical contact, excessive shoulder-charging, and pushing are heavily penalized.

3.1 Equipment & Uniform

Matches are executed utilizing a regulation **Size 5 football**. All players must wear flat-soled indoor football shoes or standard running takkies. Outdoor cleats or metal-studded boots are **strictly prohibited**.

3.2 Corner Shielding Limit

Players shielding or handling the ball in the corner circle have exactly **5 seconds** (called aloud by referee) to cleanly pass the ball or exit. Over-shielding results in immediate turnover.

3.3 The 5-Second Restart

All set-pieces and restarts (kick-ins, free kicks, corners, goalie clearance throws) must be executed within **5 seconds** from whistle. Defending walls must stand at least **5 paces (meters)** back.

4.0

GOALKEEPER REGULATIONS

⚽ Tactical Back-Pass: Strictly **one (1) back-pass** per half is permitted. Additional passes award an indirect free-kick to the opposition from the outer edge of the penalty box.

⚽ Area of Play: Hands can only be used inside the penalty box. Goalie must release/distribute the ball within **5 seconds** of gaining control.

⚽ Clean Saves: Goalkeepers can slide *only* to block a shot or save inside their penalty area, provided the challenge is clean and doesn't show studs.

⚽ Scoring: Goals scored directly by the goalkeeper must be kicked along the ground. Launching or throwing the ball by hand does not count as a valid score.

5.0

DISCIPLINARY CODE & KICKS

⚽ No Run-Up Penalty: All penalty kicks must be executed from a static, stationary stance. The non-striking foot must remain planted flat beside the ball.

🟨 Yellow Card: Carries an automatic **2-minute temporary suspension (Sin Bin)**. Team plays down a player for the duration.

🟥 Red Card: Immediate ejection for the remainder of the match. Triggers an automatic **1-game suspension** covering the next scheduled fixture.

🛑 Conduct & Referee: Fighting, verbal abuse, coarse language, or official intimidation will cause immediate expulsion. **The referee's decision is final.**
